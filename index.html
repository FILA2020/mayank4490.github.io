Research interests
 I study Computational Linguistics and Complex networks to understand and analyze the inherent behavior of scientific research articles. Particularly, I perform both content and network study on academic literature to solve some of the very pertinent problems like long-term impact prediction, network growth, popularity prediction, text reuse, etc.
